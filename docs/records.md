## World Records

World records in ManiaPlanet are the absolute best times achieved on any given map. These are tracked by the community and verified through replay files.

{: .important }
> Always save your replay files when achieving a notable time — they are required for record verification.

## Campaign Records

The official ManiaPlanet leaderboards are accessible in-game and on [ManiaPlanet](https://www.maniaplanet.com/). Rankings reset each season, but legacy times are preserved.

### Medal Thresholds

Each campaign map has four medal targets:

| Medal | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 🏆 **Author** | Beat the map creator's time |
| 🥇 **Gold** | Expert-level time |
| 🥈 **Silver** | Intermediate time |
| 🥉 **Bronze** | Completion time |

## Community Records

ManiaPlanet maintains community records for:

- Featured map collections
- Map hunting events
- Seasonal challenges

## Tracking Your Progress

Use the following tools to track your times:

- **In-game leaderboards** — Press `Scores` on any map
- **[ManiaPlanet](https://www.maniaplanet.com/)** — Comprehensive stats and progression tracking

## Record Submission

To submit a record to the ManiaPlanet community leaderboard:

1. Achieve your time and **save the replay** immediately.
2. Verify the replay is correct by watching it back.
3. Submit the replay via our Discord with:
   - Map name and UID
   - Your in-game name
   - The replay file

{: .note }
> A live community leaderboard integration is planned for a future update!
