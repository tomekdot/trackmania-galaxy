## Featured Maps

This section showcases maps highlighted by the ManiaPlanet community. Maps are reviewed for quality, creativity, and replayability.

## Campaign Maps

The official Trackmania campaigns offer a structured progression from beginner to expert. Each season introduces a new campaign with 25 maps across 5 difficulty levels:

| Difficulty | Color | Description |
|------------|-------|-------------|
| White | ⬜ | Beginner-friendly |
| Green | 🟩 | Easy |
| Blue | 🟦 | Intermediate |
| Red | 🟥 | Hard |
| Black | ⬛ | Expert |

## Map Styles

Community maps come in many styles:

- **Speedtech** — Technical maps emphasizing precise lines at high speed
- **Endurance** — Long maps testing focus and consistency
- **LOL / Kacky** — Challenging maps with quirky or frustrating gimmicks
- **Scenery / Artistic** — Visually impressive builds
- **RPG** — Puzzle-like maps with unique mechanics

## How to Find Maps

1. Open **Trackmania** and go to **My Campaigns** → **Discover Maps**
2. Browse **MX** (ManiaExchange) at [trackmania.exchange](https://trackmania.exchange/)
3. Check community recommendations in our Discord

## Map Hunting

Map hunting is the practice of achieving the best possible time on every map in a specific set (e.g., a KACKY event). Tips for map hunting:

- Focus on maps where you're close to a medal cut
- Use the [Records & Leaderboards](records) page to find gaps in your scores
- Collaborate with other hunters in the community
